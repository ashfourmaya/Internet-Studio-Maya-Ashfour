Things To Do

1. Dreamhouse Tour

Step inside Barbie’s famous Dreamhouse! Explore rooms filled with endless pink décor, fashion closets, and even a rooftop pool where every sunset sparkles. No trip to Barbie Land is complete without a tour of the iconic Barbie Dreamhouse. Step inside a world of perfect pink design — where everything is open-concept, sparkling clean, and endlessly photogenic. Each Dreamhouse reflects its Barbie’s personality, from President Barbie’s elegant office to Astronaut Barbie’s space-themed suite. There’s no need for doors or stairs — you’ll simply glide from floor to floor in true Barbie style. Peek inside the color-coordinated closets, admire the heart-shaped vanity mirrors, and don’t forget to wave from the balcony, where every sunset looks like a cotton-candy painting.

2. Dance Party at the Mojo Dojo Casa House

Join Ken and friends for the ultimate dance party! With neon lights, upbeat tunes, and mirrorball madness, the night never ends. Once the sun sets (and the glitter starts to sparkle), head to the Mojo Dojo Casa House for the ultimate dance party. Originally Ken’s makeover of his beach hangout, this spot has become the place for high-energy fun. Expect bold neon lights, horse posters galore, and a playlist that never stops — from classic Barbie pop to surf rock remixes. Everyone’s welcome to join in, whether you’re a Barbie, a Ken, or an Allan. The dance floor glows, the vibes are carefree, and the motto is simple: the more dramatic your dance moves, the better!

3. Barbie Beach Day

Catch the sun, surf the waves, or just relax under a pink umbrella. Everything’s pastel-perfect at Barbie Beach! Barbie Land’s beach isn’t just a place — it’s a lifestyle. Spend your day basking in the endless sunshine, where the sand never sticks and the waves are always picture-perfect. Join the Kens for an impromptu surf-off, play volleyball with the Barbies, or simply relax in a pink-striped cabana with a glittery (imaginary) smoothie in hand. The beach is where everyone comes together to laugh, dance, and show off their best slow-motion walks. It’s the ideal way to end your Barbie Land adventure — golden hour, good vibes, and plenty of sparkle in the air.
