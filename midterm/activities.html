Things To Do

1. Dreamhouse Tour

Step inside Barbie’s famous Dreamhouse! Explore rooms filled with endless pink décor, fashion closets, and even a rooftop pool where every sunset sparkles.

2. Dance Party at the Mojo Dojo Casa House

Join Ken and friends for the ultimate dance party! With neon lights, upbeat tunes, and mirrorball madness, the night never ends.

3. Barbie Beach Day

Catch the sun, surf the waves, or just relax under a pink umbrella. Everything’s pastel-perfect at Barbie Beach!
