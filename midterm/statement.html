Design Statement

The design of this website was inspired by the whimsical, ultra-feminine world of Barbie Land as seen in the 2023 *Barbie* movie. I chose a pink color palette to evoke feelings of joy, nostalgia, and empowerment—values that the Barbie universe represents. The use of soft blushes and hot pinks creates a vibrant yet cohesive visual identity.

The two selected fonts—Playfair Display and Inter—balance elegance and modern readability. Playfair Display adds a classic, magazine-like flair for headings, while Inter ensures clarity for paragraphs. Images were chosen to highlight the fantastical yet familiar tone of Barbie Land, blending dreamy architecture with beach and party elements.

Buttons are rounded and bright to mimic Barbie’s iconic branding. Placement prioritizes accessibility, with CTAs visible above and below key sections. The overall design focuses on user engagement, mirroring the film’s themes of imagination and individuality.
