Places To Eat

1. The Dream Diner

Classic American vibes meet a pink aesthetic. Pancakes, milkshakes, and fun retro booths await!

2. Café Malibu

Enjoy lattes topped with heart-shaped foam and light lunches by the beachside, surrounded by pastel umbrellas.

3. Ken’s Grill

Ken’s favorite spot for BBQ with a side of style. Try the pink lemonade while relaxing to beach tunes.
