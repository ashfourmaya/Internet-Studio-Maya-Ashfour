My 12 Favorite Movies

The Matrix
I love the mind-bending sci-fi concept and the revolutionary action scenes.
Inception
The layered dream worlds and emotional story make it unforgettable.
Interstellar
Beautiful visually and emotionally—one of the best sci-fi epics ever made.
The Dark Knight
Iconic performances and deep storytelling make this superhero film stand out.
Forrest Gump
Heartwarming, funny, and inspiring—Forrest's journey never gets old.
Pulp Fiction
I love the quirky dialogue and clever nonlinear storytelling.
The Lord of the Rings
A masterpiece of world-building, adventure, and emotional depth.
Star Wars: A New Hope
A timeless space adventure that shaped modern sci-fi.
Gladiator
Epic action and a powerful story of honor and revenge.
Back to the Future
Fun, clever, and endlessly rewatchable—a perfect adventure.
Avatar
The world of Pandora is stunning and deeply immersive.
The Shawshank Redemption
Hope, friendship, and redemption—it's nearly perfect storytelling.
