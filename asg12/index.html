My Top 12 Movies

These movies are my go-to comfort watches. Rom-coms make me laugh and remind me how fun and unpredictable love can be, while Disney movies bring nostalgia, adventure, and heartfelt messages that still resonate with me today. This collection mixes stories that lift my mood, inspire me, and make me smile every time I re-watch them.

10 Things I Hate About You
I love the witty banter and the iconic teen-romance charm.
The Proposal
Sandra Bullock and Ryan Reynolds have hilarious chemistry.
Crazy Rich Asians
A modern, colorful fairy tale with unforgettable romance.
La La Land
Beautiful music and a bittersweet love story make it special.
When Harry Met Sally
A classic with timeless humor and deep emotional moments.
27 Dresses
It’s sweet, funny, and Katherine Heigl is effortlessly charming.
The Lion King
A powerful story about courage, family, and destiny.
Frozen
Catchy songs and a beautiful story about sisterhood.
Moana
Empowering, visually stunning, and full of great music.
Beauty and the Beast
A magical tale that I’ve loved since childhood.
Mulan
A fearless heroine and an inspiring story of bravery.
Tangled
A fun, heartfelt adventure with amazing animation.
